About

I'm just a clever problem-solver with lots of experience and a drive to be excellent.

Throughout my career I've worked on a diverse set of titles in a multitude of spaces with tech stacks ranging from in-engine UI and gameplay to RESTful and event-driven game platform microservices. I've worked on a variety of teams in collaboration with QA, product, design, and delivery; and I pride myself in my total-ownership mindset and precise execution.

As my direct reports will tell you, I'm fond of saying that a great engineer doesn't need to know how to do everything, but rather what makes an engineer great is the ability to figure anything out. My approach to problem solving relies on efficient use of the resources and support available to me, as well as leveraging a decade of personal experience in game engineering.

My career has taken me from mobile game development to console/HD sports titles, to game platform development specializing in player dynamics. I've been an engineer for many years, and have recently embarked on a new journey into engineering management. While proud of my direct contributions, I've also learned that my dedication and passion are paramount to the success of those around me. I aim to empower and lead by example to force-multiply my entire team.

I'm eager to show you what I can do for your team and your games. I promise that you won't be disappointed.
